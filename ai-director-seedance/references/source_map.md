Use for:

- blocking-first camera placement

URL:

- https://theasc.com/articles/shot-craft-where-do-you-put-the-camera

Use for:

- choosing movement based on shot purpose

## What does not count as a primary source

Do not treat these as primary authority:

- random prompt lists
- individual social posts
- generic “cinematic prompt” blog posts
- unmaintained hobby repos with no evidence of professional workflow grounding

Community tools are acceptable only when they provide implementation hints that do not conflict with the primary sources.
